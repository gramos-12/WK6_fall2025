TLC Coffee & Tea


Who We Are


We are an independent, family owned coffee roaster and cafe'. We have been at our Fremont location for over 35 years. We believe is supporting our community. Some of our community contributions include yearly college scholarships and sponsoring local youth sports leagues. Our sincere belief is to give back to the community that supports us. Our energetic and friendly staff is waiting to serve you.



Our Philosophy


We are waiting to serve you the best coffee or tea. Stop by and enjoy our freshly brewed coffees & teas. We take pride in serving the perfect brew to start your day or for an afternoon break. Premium coffee, teas and exceptional experiences await you. Check out our menu for additional items served.



Our Commitment


Our number one commitment is to serve the the best quality in all that we serve. We take pride in the large variety of coffees and teas we serve. Our pastries are baked daily to provide you with the best quality and flavor. We have created a relaxing and welcoming atmosphere for you to enjoy your daily brew.
